Training

The FSS offers an extensive range of Forensic Science training solutions covering both the public and private sector, primarily supporting the UK Criminal Justice System.

Events are designed and delivered by accredited forensic practitioners and tailored specifically to meet the needs of professionals involved in criminal or civil forensic investigation.

For further information please contact us.

Additional Services:

  • FORS: Forensic Bibliographic Database
    This Forensic Science Service® FORS® database is multidisciplinary and covers literature relevant to the examination of evidential materials, analytical methods and the presentation and interpretation of findings.
  • FORSight: The Forensic Abstracts Journal
    This service provides rapid, comprehensive current awareness for forensic scientists and other professionals involved in crime investigation, medical and legal occupations.
