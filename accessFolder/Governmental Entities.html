Focusing on Governmental Entities

Moore & Cubbedge offers a full array of services and solutions designed to support the business of government. We know that local government is the heart of every community, and we will deliver the tools and knowledge you require to keep your community thriving. Trust Moore & Cubbedge to provide the support you need to navigate the increasingly complex landscape of governmental finance.

Our blend of traditional accounting, tax, and management consulting services combined with outstanding customer service ensure the unique needs of governmental organizations are met. Our seasoned team of experienced professionals is responsive to your requests, delivering information that is timely and reliable.

For over 30 years, Moore & Cubbedge has been servicing governmental entities such as county government, city government, authorities, and health departments. Whether you’re ready to assemble a team or simply looking for more information on our services,  we’re eager to listen. Contact us today.
